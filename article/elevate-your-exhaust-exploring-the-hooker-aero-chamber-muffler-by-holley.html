The Hooker Aero Chamber Muffler is made from 16-gauge aluminized steel, which is durable and resistant to corrosion. The muffler has a unique step principle, similar to that used in racing header design, which increases the exhaust gas velocity and flow. The muffler also has an aerodynamic lens that directs the sound pressure waves into an acceleration chamber and out of the muffler, creating a powerful sound that will not change over time. The muffler also has a resonance chamber and a tuning tube that cancel unwanted frequencies and amplitudes, eliminating the annoying tin can sound or drone at cruising speed. The muffler also has a stainless steel perforated internal plate that prevents internal rust, and reinforced inlets and outlets for added strength.

The Hooker Aero Chamber Muffler is available in different sizes and configurations, depending on your vehicle and preference.
