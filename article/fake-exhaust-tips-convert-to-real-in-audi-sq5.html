Fake exhaust tips convert to real in Audi SQ5

The Audi SQ5 is a powerful and elegant SUV, but many owners dislike its fake exhaust tips. Instead of functional outlets, the B9 and B9.5 SQ5 models often have plastic covers hiding the real pipes that point downward. Luckily, there’s a popular solution: converting to real exhaust tips using the OEM Audi Sport part number 80A-071-904.

Why upgrade fake exhaust tips to real?

  • Better, authentic look – real pipes instead of covers
  • Improved exhaust sound thanks to perforated chambers in OEM tips
  • Higher resale value for enthusiasts

Installation tips from Audi SQ5 owners

Owners from forums like Audizine shared many helpful tips:

  • Installation can take 4–6 hours if you do it carefully
  • Use a drill, small saw, Dremel, and file to cut the rear valance
  • Be patient – the plastic valance can crack or look messy if rushed
  • The driver-side muffler exit is angled upward, so the left tip may look crooked – fix by carefully bending the pipe down with vice grips
  • Do not leave the lower valance plastic near the tips – it may melt

Should you do it yourself or go to a shop?

Many owners recommend doing it yourself if you have tools and patience. General exhaust shops might do a quick job that looks uneven. Some owners finished in one hour, but for the best result, take your time.

Does it change the exhaust sound?

Yes! The OEM tips have internal perforations and act like mini resonators. Forum users say the sound becomes deeper, louder, and more refined without being too aggressive.

One owner wrote: “These tips hugely change exhaust note—much deeper and rumblier and a bit louder. It’s the best sounding exhaust I’ve ever heard on the EA839 3.0T.”

Fitment notes and model years

  • Part number 80A-071-904 is officially for B9.5 SQ5 (2021+)
  • For B9 SQ5 (2018–2020) you may need to cut the valance
  • Some tried replacing the lower bumper with B9.5 part – it doesn’t fit directly

Other alternatives

  • Buy cheaper tips from Alibaba and weld custom extensions
  • Have a muffler shop cut and bend pipes to fit
  • Powder coat tips black for a custom look

Is it worth it?

Most owners say yes: real tips look better, improve sound, and add value. OEM tips are expensive, but many feel the quality and design justify the price.

Summary: how to convert fake to real tips on your Audi SQ5

  • Confirm your model year – easier on B9.5 (2021+)
  • Buy OEM part 80A-071-904
  • Cut valance carefully if needed
  • Adjust pipes to avoid crooked look
  • Enjoy the real exhaust look and better sound!
