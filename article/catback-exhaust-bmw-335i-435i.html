Catback Exhaust for your 335i & 435i (N55 Engine)

If you're looking to upgrade your BMW 335i or 435i, a catback exhaust system is a great place to start. The VRSF 3" Catback Exhaust is a popular choice for BMW enthusiasts looking to improve the performance and sound of their vehicle.

Made from High-Quality Materials

The VRSF catback exhaust system is made from the highest quality 304 grade, mandrel bent stainless steel. This ensures maximum durability and resistance to corrosion over time. Additionally, VRSF includes super-flex, stainless steel band clamps to make installation quick and easy.

Increased Performance

One of the biggest benefits of installing a catback exhaust system is the increase in performance. By increasing the overall diameter of the piping to 3", back pressure is significantly reduced which results in faster spool and more power. You'll notice a significant improvement in throttle response and overall performance.

Distinctive Sound

The VRSF catback exhaust system is designed to produce a deep, distinctive exhaust note at wide open throttle. However, at idle, the street muffler's exhaust note is subtle and slightly louder than stock. You'll appreciate the improved sound without sacrificing daily drivability.

Features and Benefits

  • Made from hand crafted, tig welded 3" stainless steel.
  • 100% 304 grade stainless steel construction.
  • Over 22lbs of weight savings over the stock exhaust!
  • Fully adjustable/replaceable tip design.
  • Mandrel bent, smooth radius bends to decrease turbulence and increase flow.
  • VRSF "No Hassle" Lifetime Warranty.

Compatibility

The VRSF 3" Catback Exhaust is designed to fit the following BMWs (N55 Engine):

  • 2012 - 2018 F30 / F31 BMW 335i & 335i xDrive
  • 2012 - 2018 F32 / F36 BMW 435i & 435i xDrive

With its high-quality construction, improved performance, and distinctive sound, you'll love the way your BMW looks and feels with this upgrade. Plus, with VRSF's "No Hassle" Lifetime Warranty, you can rest assured that your investment is protected.
