The ValvedExhausts catback system for the Toyota Supra A90/A91 comes with several features that make it an excellent choice for car enthusiasts. Some of these features include:

The ValvedExhausts catback system has vacuum actuated valves that allow you to control the sound of your car with a remote. This means you can switch between a quiet mode for daily driving and a loud mode for track days or when you want to show off.

3 Inch Pipe Diameter

The catback system has a 3-inch pipe diameter, which allows for better exhaust flow and improved performance. With the larger pipe diameter, the exhaust gases can exit the engine more efficiently, resulting in reduced backpressure and increased horsepower.

Lighter than the OEM exhaust

The ValvedExhausts catback system is also lighter than the OEM exhaust, which can help improve your car's handling and overall performance. By reducing the weight at the rear of the vehicle, you can experience improved cornering and better weight distribution, making your Toyota Supra feel more agile on the road or track.

Fully Clamp-On Exhaust (No Welding Needed)

The catback system is fully clamp-on, which means you don't need to weld it onto your car. This makes installation easier and quicker. Additionally, the clamp-on design ensures that you can easily remove the exhaust system if needed without causing any damage to your vehicle.

100% Reversible

The ValvedExhausts catback system is 100% reversible, which means you can always go back to the stock exhaust at any time. Whether you plan to sell your Toyota Supra or want to switch to a different aftermarket exhaust in the future, having the option to revert to the stock setup is a significant advantage.

Minimal Drone Experience

The catback system also offers a minimal drone experience, which means you won't experience excessive noise or vibration in the cabin. Drone, a low-frequency noise produced by exhaust systems at certain RPM ranges, can be a nuisance during long drives. With the ValvedExhausts catback system, you can enjoy a pleasant and comfortable driving experience without the annoyance of excessive drone noise.
