What is a Valved Exhaust?

A valved exhaust is an aftermarket upgrade that allows you to adjust the sound of your car's exhaust system. By opening or closing the valves in the exhaust system, you can change the volume and tone of the exhaust note, making it louder or quieter, deeper or raspier, depending on your preferences.
