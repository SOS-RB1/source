MAGNAFLOW 2021-2024 RAM 1500 XMOD SERIES CAT-BACK PERFORMANCE EXHAUST SYSTEM

The MagnaFlow Ram TRX xMOD Cat-Back Performance Exhaust System #19552 is designed to enhance the performance and sound of your 2021-2024 Ram 1500 TRX. Known for its versatility, this exhaust system provides a configurable exhaust sound that can be tailored to your preferences. Each #19552 kit incorporates MagnaFlow’s No Drone Technology (NDT) to minimize unwanted noise at lower RPMs while delivering a deep, signature exhaust tone in any configuration.

A key feature of the NDT is the swappable NDT Delete module, which uses ¼-wave passive noise cancellation to ensure quiet highway driving while allowing for a robust sound under acceleration. The kit also includes two additional interchangeable xMOD modules: the xTreme Deletes and Muffler Modules. The xTreme Deletes module offers a straight pipe, unmuffled exhaust sound for a bold, aggressive tone, while the Muffler Modules provide a deep and powerful, yet more subdued sound, utilizing MagnaFlow’s straight-through muffler design.

These modules are engineered for dyno-proven performance and can be easily swapped thanks to racing-bred v-band clamps. The #19552 exhaust system features mandrel bent 3” tubing with a dual exit configuration and black double-wall powder-coated 5” tips. It is constructed with 100% stainless steel using CNC precision robotic manufacturing and is backed by MagnaFlow’s limited lifetime warranty. Ensure compliance with local noise regulations.
