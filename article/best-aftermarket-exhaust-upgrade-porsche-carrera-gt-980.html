1. Kline Innovation Exhaust

  • High-flow HJS race cats or de-cat pipes for improved exhaust flow.
  • Signature 1D CLR x-pipe for an authentic F1 sound.
  • Valve-controlled system: open for an aggressive scream, closed for reduced drone.
  • Available in stainless steel (21kg) or lightweight Inconel 625 (11kg).
  • Power gains: +14 HP with race cats, +20 HP with de-cat pipes.

2. JCR Developments Inconel Valved Race Pipe

  • Constructed from 100% Inconel 625for durability and weight reduction.
  • Retains OEM valve controlfor quiet cruising or aggressive sound.
  • Significant weight savings: 19.98kg lighter than stock.
  • Performance gains: +13 HP and +11.4 Nm torque.
  • Comes with a bespoke storage box for the stock exhaust.

3. Repasi Motorwerks Exhaust

  • Designed to retain the F1-derived pitch, enhancing volume.
  • Minimalistic, elegant stainless steel construction.
  • Weight reduction: saves nearly 50 lbs (22.7 kg) from stock.
  • Flex pipe section ensures perfect fitment and easy installation.
  • Price: $8,980.

4. Boden Autohaus Titanium Exhaust

  • Weight: Only 5.7 lbs (92% lighter than stock).
  • Titanium x-pipe design enhances the high-pitched V10 scream.
  • Precision-fit for easy bolt-on installation.
  • Custom hand-built craftsmanship.

5. Kreissieg F1 Sound Valvetronic Exhaust

  • Stainless steel construction for durability.
  • Valvetronic system allows switching between different sound levels.
  • Customizable sound levels (Levels 1-5), with Level 5 offering a true high-pitched F1 tone.
  • Retains OEM tips and fitment.

6. Cargraphic Sport Rear Silencer

  • Stainless steel build with integrated exhaust flaps.
  • Three flap control settings: Always Open, Always Closed, Auto (RPM-dependent).
  • Deep, full exhaust tone for a balanced mix of aggression and refinement.
  • Price: €7,134 (including VAT).

7. GMG WC-Sport Exhaust System

  • Developed with F1 silencers and patent-pending crossover design.
  • +19 wheel horsepower and +16 wheel torque.
  • Works with GIAC-tuned ECU for optimal performance.
  • Constructed from high-quality T304 stainless steel.
  • Aerospace hardware and F1 slip-fit design for a perfect seal.

The best exhaustfor your Porsche Carrera GT depends on your priorities:
