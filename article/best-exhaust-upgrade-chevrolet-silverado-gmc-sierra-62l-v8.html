Best Exhaust Upgrade for Chevrolet Silverado / GMC Sierra 6.2L V8 (2019+, 4th Generation)

If you own a 2019 or newer Chevrolet Silverado or GMC Sierra equipped with the mighty 6.2L V8, chances are you’re not just looking for utility — you want performance, personality, and that deep V8 rumble that turns heads and makes every drive an experience. One of the most effective upgrades for achieving that is a high-quality cat-back exhaust system.

This guide compares the top exhaust upgrades for the 4th-generation Silverado and Sierra 6.2L V8 — based on performance, sound, materials, fitment, and value.

1. CORSA Performance Sport Cat-Back (Part #21200)

  • Price: $1,848.99
  • Configuration: 3.0” to 2.5” dual rear exit with twin 4.0” polished or black tips
  • Sound Profile: Sport
  • Performance Gains: +17 HP / +16 lb-ft / +47% flow increase
  • Material: Premium stainless steel
  • Warranty: Limited lifetime

CORSA offers a refined yet aggressive tone with drone-canceling technology. It’s one of the highest-quality options on the market.

2. Borla ATAK Cat-Back (Part #140998)

  • Price: $1,758.99
  • Configuration: 3.5” to dual 2.75” with bright chrome tips
  • Sound Profile: Aggressive
  • Technology: Polyphonic Harmonizer and Valve Simulator
  • Material: T-304 stainless steel
  • Warranty: Million-Mile Warranty

Perfect for those who want an aggressive sound. This system includes advanced harmonizing tech and drone management.

3. MagnaFlow Overland Series (Part #19626)

  • Price: $1,596
  • Configuration: 3.5” piping to dual rear high-clearance exit
  • Sound Profile: Tuned for off-road and camp-friendly tone
  • Technology: No Drone Technology (NDT)
  • Material: Stainless steel
  • Warranty: Limited Lifetime

This system is built for explorers, offering both performance and comfort over long journeys.

4. AWE 0FG Cat-Back

  • Price: Around $1,800+
  • Configuration: Dual 3” T304L stainless, quad 4” tips
  • Sound Profile: Deep and refined
  • Material: U.S.-sourced T304L stainless steel
  • Warranty: Lifetime

AWE’s drone-canceling 180 Technology® makes this one of the best sounding exhausts available for the 6.2L V8.

5. aFe POWER Vulcan Series

  • Price: $1,420.20
  • Configuration: 4" to dual 3” stainless with quad 4" polished tips
  • Sound Profile: Aggressive
  • Material: 304 stainless steel
  • Warranty: Manufacturer’s warranty

Great value and excellent performance make this a standout choice for those wanting a bold look and sound.

6. MBRP Armor Lite Cat-Back (Part #S5065AL)

  • Price: Around $600–700
  • Configuration: 3” to 2.5” aluminized steel
  • Sound Profile: Street
  • Material: Aluminized steel
  • Warranty: 3-Year

Best for budget-conscious buyers. Not stainless steel, but still a solid tone improvement over stock.

7. Flowmaster FlowFX (Part #717890)

  • Price: $665.95
  • Configuration: 3.5” to dual 2.5” mandrel-bent with OEM tips
  • Sound Profile: Moderate
  • Material: 409 stainless steel
  • Warranty: Lifetime Limited

A solid all-around performer. Good power gains and moderate sound with a factory appearance.

Conclusion

The best exhaust depends on your needs:

  • For aggressive sound: Borla ATAK or AWE 0FG
  • For refined performance: CORSA Sport or MagnaFlow Overland
  • For budget: Flowmaster or MBRP
  • For flash and quad tips: aFe Vulcan
