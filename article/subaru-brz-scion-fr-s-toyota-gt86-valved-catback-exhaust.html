Subaru BRZ or Scion FR-S (Toyota GT86) Valved Cat Back Exhaust

Introduction

Are you looking to elevate your driving experience with your Subaru BRZ, Scion FR-S, or Toyota GT86? A valved cat back exhaust system is an excellent choice for car enthusiasts seeking improved performance and sound. This type of exhaust system offers numerous benefits, including increased horsepower and torque, enhanced exhaust note, and reduced weight. In this article, we will delve into the advantages of a valved cat back exhaust system and explain why it's a compelling upgrade for your Subaru BRZ or Scion FR-S (Toyota GT86).

Advantages of a Valved Cat Back Exhaust System

Increased Performance

Installing a valved cat back exhaust system can yield significant performance improvements for your Subaru BRZ or Scion FR-S (Toyota GT86). By reducing exhaust system backpressure, the cat back exhaust enhances engine efficiency, leading to increased horsepower and torque. These power gains can be even more remarkable when combined with other performance upgrades like an intake or tune. Additionally, the valved feature allows you to control the exhaust sound, enabling a quieter note when desired, making it a versatile enhancement for both the street and the track.

Improved Sound

The auditory aspect of driving is crucial to any automotive enthusiast. A valved cat back exhaust system takes the sound experience to a whole new level in your Subaru BRZ or Scion FR-S (Toyota GT86). This exhaust system produces a deeper, more aggressive exhaust note, giving your car a sporty and powerful sound signature. The valved feature enables sound customization, allowing you to enjoy a subtle exhaust note when needed. The enhanced exhaust sound not only makes your driving experience more enjoyable but also turns heads wherever you go, adding an element of excitement to your journeys.

Reduced Weight

Apart from the performance and sound advantages, a valved cat back exhaust system also contributes to weight reduction in your Subaru BRZ or Scion FR-S (Toyota GT86). Available in both stainless steel and titanium construction, the titanium option offers a lighter alternative. This weight reduction translates to faster acceleration, improved handling, and enhanced fuel efficiency for your vehicle. Moreover, the reduced weight can have a positive impact on the longevity of other critical components like the brakes and suspension, ensuring your car performs at its best for years to come.
