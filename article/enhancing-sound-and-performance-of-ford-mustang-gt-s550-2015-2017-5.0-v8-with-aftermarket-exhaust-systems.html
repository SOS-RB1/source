Enhancing Sound and Performance of Ford Mustang GT S550 2015-2017 5.0 V8 with Aftermarket Exhaust Systems

Ford Mustang GT 2015-2017 with a 5.0 V8 engine is an icon of power and style. However, for enthusiasts seeking an enhanced engine sound, the factory exhaust system may not always deliver the desired experience. In this article, we will explore aftermarket exhaust systems, focusing on three manufacturers: CORSA Performance, ValvedExhaust, and ARMYTRIX.

Various Sound Configurations:

To witness the impact of different configurations on the Mustang GT's sound, we recommend watching a video featuring 61 sound samples. Below, you'll find samples for configurations such as axle-back, cat-back, muffler delete, resonator delete, x-pipe, and h-pipe.

Benefits of Aftermarket Exhaust Systems:

  • Improved Sound: Aftermarket exhaust significantly enhances the engine sound, providing a deeper, louder, and more aggressive tone. Sound adjustment through valves allows customization for various driving conditions.
  • Increased Power: Enhancing exhaust gas flow through an aftermarket system translates to increased engine power and torque. Removing certain components, such as mufflers or resonators, can positively impact performance.
  • Enhanced Appearance: The exhaust system also influences the car's appearance, adding a sporty character. Choosing different exhaust tips, pipes, and bends enables customization based on individual preferences.

Tips for Choosing an Exhaust:

  • Material and Construction Quality: Ensure the exhaust is made of high-quality stainless steel or titanium to provide durability and resistance to atmospheric conditions.
  • Compliance with Laws and Standards: Check if the chosen exhaust complies with regulations regarding noise, emissions, and other standards applicable in the region.
  • Price and Value: Compare various offers, considering both the price and the quality of the product. The most expensive is not always the best, and the cheapest is not necessarily the worst.
