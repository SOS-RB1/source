Best Exhaust Upgrades for 2020-2024 Toyota GR Yaris

The Toyota GR Yaris has quickly become a favorite among car enthusiasts, known for its rally-inspired performance and tunability. One of the most impactful modifications you can make to this pocket rocket is upgrading the exhaust system. Not only does it enhance the car's auditory experience, but it also contributes to performance gains. Let's explore some of the top aftermarket exhaust options available for the GR Yaris.

Akrapovič Slip-On Race Line (Titanium) and Evolution Link Pipe (SS)

Akrapovič is synonymous with high-end performance exhausts, and their Slip-On Race Line for the GR Yaris is no exception. Made from titanium, this exhaust system is lightweight and offers a significant improvement in flow. The accompanying Evolution Link Pipe, made from stainless steel, ensures a smooth exhaust gas flow that's crucial for both performance and sound. The result is a deep, sporty rumble that's music to the ears of any car enthusiast.

Part number: S-TY/T/2, E-TY/SS/3

Price: 2,220.83€, 420.44€

QuickSilver Exhaust with Sound Architect

QuickSilver's solution for the GR Yaris comes with their innovative Sound Architect technology. This allows drivers to control the exhaust note via a smartphone app, making it possible to switch between a subdued tone for daily driving and an aggressive roar on demand. The system is designed for easy installation and promises a noticeable bump in horsepower.

Part number: TY088S

Price: £1,814.99

HKS LEGAMAX Premium

HKS, a well-respected name in the tuning world, offers the LEGAMAX Premium exhaust for the GR Yaris. It strikes a balance between performance and street legality, providing a sporty sound that's not overwhelming. Armytrix Valvetronic Exhaust System

Armytrix is known for their valvetronic exhaust systems, which give drivers the ability to adjust the exhaust valve positions. The system for the GR Yaris includes a remote control to switch between different modes, offering a customizable experience. Whether you're looking for a stealthy ride or an all-out auditory assault, the Armytrix system delivers.

Part number: JQR20202129

Price: ¥202,400

Cobra Cat Turbo Back Performance Exhaust

Cobra's Cat Turbo Back system for the GR Yaris is designed to maximize exhaust flow, which is key for turbocharged engines. By replacing the entire exhaust from the turbocharger back, this system reduces backpressure and allows the engine to breathe more freely, translating to improved throttle response and power.

Part number: TY32ATP19

Price: £1,333.33

FI-Exhaust OPF / Non-OPF Exhaust System

Frequency Intelligent Exhaust offers a versatile system that caters to both OPF and non-OPF equipped GR Yaris models. Their exhaust systems are known for enhancing the sound and performance of the vehicle, and this particular model includes valves to adjust the exhaust note according to the driver's preference.

Part number: TO-GRY-CBE + ACT-BN + TIP63127S*2 + R2016OL + OBD-CAB-5BNE

Price: $2,500.00

Ragazzon Carbon 2x 100 mm Ragazzon EVO Line

Ragazzon's EVO Line exhaust for the GR Yaris features dual 100 mm carbon fiber tips for a distinctive look. The Italian brand is known for its craftsmanship and attention to detail, and this exhaust system is no exception. It provides a sporty tone and improved exhaust flow, complementing the GR Yaris's dynamic character.

Upgrading the exhaust system on your Toyota GR Yaris can transform the car's performance and driving experience.
