Unleash Your BMW F80 M3 / F82 M4 True Potential with the Rear Exhaust Section

If you're looking for a way to improve the sound and performance of your BMW F8X M3 or M4, the Rear Exhaust Section - 3 Can Valve is the perfect solution. This carefully engineered rear exhaust section is designed to replace the factory M3 and M4 exhaust and offers a deep, throaty growl that perfectly complements the car's power and style.

The Active Autowerke Maad Maxx Rear Exhaust Section - 3 Can Valve F8X BMW M3 & M4 2015 - 2020 addresses the lackluster sound of the stock OEM exhaust and delivers optimum flow and resonance for a more powerful performance. It features flex joints to reduce vibration and a double-canned muffler to reduce resonance, resulting in a low, deep rumble that is sure to turn heads.
