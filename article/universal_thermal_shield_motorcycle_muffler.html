How to Protect Yourself from Motorcycle Exhaust Burns? Install a Universal Thermal Shield on Your Motorcycle Muffler

Introduction

Riding a motorcycle is a pleasurable activity that can quickly turn into a nightmare if you get burned by the exhaust system. The motorcycle exhaust system consists of a muffler and a pipe that carries hot gases from the engine. The exhaust pipe can get very hot and cause burns to your skin or clothing. However, there is a simple solution to protect yourself from burns - install a thermal shield on your motorcycle muffler.

Importance of a Thermal Shield

A thermal shield is a protective cover that is mounted on the motorcycle muffler to prevent heat from escaping and burning anyone or anything that comes into contact with it. The thermal shield is made of heat-resistant material that can withstand high temperatures and prevent burns from the exhaust pipe.

2 Color Options to Choose From

The shield is available in two color options: shiny chrome and matte black. Shiny chrome is a glossy finish that can give your motorcycle a stylish look, while matte black is a more subtle finish that can be matched to your motorcycle's design.

Installation of the Thermal Shield

The installation of the thermal shield on your motorcycle muffler is a simple process that can be done by yourself. It is important to ensure that the thermal shield is properly installed to prevent it from falling off during the ride.

Conclusion

In summary, installing a thermal shield on your motorcycle muffler is a simple and effective way to protect yourself from burns caused by the exhaust system. By taking this simple step, you can enjoy riding your motorcycle without worrying about burns from the exhaust system.
