The Importance of Exhaust Systems for Two-Stroke Motorcycles

Two-stroke engines are commonly found in motorcycles, particularly in off-road and racing models. These engines work differently than four-stroke engines, as they use a different combustion cycle to generate power. Because of this, two-stroke engines require a specific type of exhaust system to operate efficiently and effectively.
